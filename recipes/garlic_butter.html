Garlic Butter

Description

Tasty garlic butter!

Original recipe

Per Serving: 448 calories; protein 29.7g; carbohydrates 36.5g; fat 21.3g; cholesterol 81.8mg; sodium 1400.4mg.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon ground paprika

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, combine softened butter, minced garlic and parmesan cheese.

  2. Season with garlic salt, Italian seasoning, pepper and paprika. Mix until smooth.
